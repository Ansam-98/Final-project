ABOUT US

The Ministry of Tourism and Antiquities is the authority concerned with leading tourism development in Jordan, supporting and directing efforts to promote tourism locally and internationally, develop tourism services, stimulate investment in the tourism field, and employ the Kingdom's rich and varied archaeological, historical, natural and cultural heritage, and it is the competent authority to license tourism agencies and associations in Jordan. Tourism is a major tributary of the Jordanian economy, as the country is rich in archaeological and natural places. The Ministry of Tourism in Jordan seeks to improve the quality of the tourism product to attract the largest number of visitors and tourists from Jordan and the countries of the world, and several legislations and laws have been enacted to serve the tourism sector.
